Buckle Up, Wolverines Fans: ESPN Plus Decoded

ESPN Plus, the streaming service that boasts it has "more live sports than you can handle" (their words, not mine), might seem like the perfect solution. But before you hit subscribe and crack open that celebratory cold one, there's a little wrinkle to iron out.

ESPN Plus is fantastic for catching obscure underwater basket weaving championships and competitive tiddlywinks tournaments (hey, don't judge, they're intense!). Don't Despair, Maize and Blue Faithful! There's Still Hope

Fear not, fellow fanatic! Here's the good news:

  • Sometimes, ESPN Plus does simulcast games airing on ESPN. So, if the Michigan game is blessed with this magical simulcast status, you're good to go with your ESPN Plus subscription.
  • The ESPN app (which comes with a cable subscription) usually lets you stream games airing on ESPN channels. So, if you have cable and the ESPN app, that's another route to cheer on your Wolverines.

Pro Tip: Check the TV listings or the ESPN app before kickoff to see where the Michigan game is airing.

You've Got Questions, We've Got (Hopefully) Answers: ESPN Plus and the Michigan Game FAQ

Still scratching your head about how to catch the game? Don't worry, we've got you covered with a quick FAQ:

How to find out where the Michigan game is airing?

Check your local TV listings or the ESPN app.

How to tell if the Michigan game is simulcast on ESPN Plus?

The ESPN schedule or app will usually indicate if a game is being simulcast.

How to subscribe to ESPN Plus (just in case)?

You can subscribe directly through the ESPN app or website.

How many devices can I stream on ESPN Plus?

Up to three devices simultaneously.

How much is an ESPN Plus subscription?
