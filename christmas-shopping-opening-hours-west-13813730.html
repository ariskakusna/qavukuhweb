Christmas shopping opening hours for west London shopping centres

Publish date: 2024-08-25

There's no denying it, London is one of the go-to destinations for those who want to shop 'til we drop.

So when it comes to Christmas, you are not short of places to go to pick up gifts for your loved ones and a few treats for yourself.

Here is all you need to know about the Christmas opening hours at west London's shopping centres.

After more festive fun?

Brent Cross

Where: Hendon, London, NW4 3FP

The shopping centre is home to more than 2,000 leading and designer brands and 120 stores including Fenwick and John Lewis.

Parking is free so you can shop all day.

Opening times:

From Thursday November 30

  • The centre will be open until 10pm Monday to Saturday.

From Thursday December 14

  • Shops will be open until 11pm Monday to Saturday.

Brent Cross will be open from 11am every Sunday in December although many bigger stores will open from 12pm. The centre will be closed on Christmas Day and New Year's Day. Opening hours are back to normal after Tuesday January 2.

Westfield

Where: Ariel Way, Shepherds Bush, London, W12 7GF

With High Street chains and luxury retailers all located within one centre, you're guaranteed to leave with something, no matter what your budget is.

Designers stores include Burberry, Boss, Gucci, Louis Vuitton, Versace and Jimmy Choo.

Opening times:

Shops will be open from 10am - 11pm from Monday to Friday and from 12pm- 6pm on Sundays during December.

  • Christmas Eve: 12pm-6pm
  • Christmas Day: Closed
  • Boxing Day: 7am-11pm
  • Wednesday December 28- 29 shops will be open from 9am-11pm

Parking

The car park is open 6am - 3am, 7 days a week.

Standard Parking Charges:

  • 0 - 1 Hours: £2.90
  • 1 - 2 Hours: £5.80
  • 2 - 24 Hours: £8.00

Intu Uxbridge

Where: High Street, Uxbridge, UB8 1LA

Opening times:

Intu Uxbridge is open 9am-8pm from Monday to Friday during December and on Sundays open from 11am-5pm.

  • Christmas Eve - 11am-5pm
  • Christmas Day - Closed
  • Boxing Day - 10am-6pm
  • New Years Eve - 11am-5pm

Parking: Intu Uxbridge has a multi-storey car park with more than 1,500 spaces.

For two hours it costs £2, and up to 10 hours cost £10.

Ealing Broadway

Where: Ealing Broadway, London, W5 5JY

Retailers in the centre include Marks & Spencer, H&M, River Island, Next and Boots.

Opening times:

From December 11-23, shops will be open from 9am-8pm Monday to Friday and 11am-5pm on Sunday.

  • Christmas Eve - 11am-5pm
  • Christmas Day - Closed
  • Boxing Day - 10am-6pm
  • New Years Eve - 11am-5pm
  • New Years Day - 10am-6pm

Parking:

There is space for over 600 cars from Monday to Friday and over 800 cars every weekend at the Ealing Broadway Shopping Centre

The car park can be reached from either Grove Road via St Mary’s Road (opposite Ealing Green) or Windsor Road via Ealing Common.

Prices start from £1 an hour and for five- six hours costs £5.

Harvey Nichols

Harvey Nichols is a luxury British department store chain with its flagship store in Knightsbridge.

It sells fashion collections for men and women, fashion accessories, beauty products, wine and food- so there is something for everyone.

Where: Knightsbridge, London, SW1X 7RJ

  • Christmas Eve - 11.30am-6pm
  • Christmas Day - Closed
  • Boxing Day - 10am-8pm
  • New Year's Eve - 11.30am-6pm
  • New Year's Day - 11.30am-7pm

Parking:

We recommend using public transport to get to Harvey Nichols.
