Cash Stunt

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Atlanta rapper Villa drops off his latest offering on his #MDNGHTMASS series, 'Cash Stunt'. Villa's auto-tuned melodic flow floats over the smooth trap production as he sheds bars on keeping his eyes on the prize. 'Cash Stunt'  has Villa staying on top of his game, making sure that his hustle shines above all else. 

It's a strong effort that builds off of the theme of MTW which was also produced by Sunday License. Clearly his production sparks the hustle mentality in Villa's pen. 

With the continuation of his #MDNGHTMASS series, Villa is bound to have more hustler anthems for the streets.
