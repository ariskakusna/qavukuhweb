Limbless pastor Nick Vujicic shares beautiful family photo

Publish date: 2024-08-20

- Australian Christian evangelist Nick Vujicic, who was born limbless and his wife Kanae have the internet mushy over their adorable family photo

- The evangelist shared the cutest photo of himself and his family as they celebrate Thanksgiving Day

- Vujicic accompanied the post with a note filled with thanks for all the good things in their lives

Limbless Australian Christian evangelist Nick Vujicic and his wife Kanae recently released the most adorable photos of their family as they celebrated Thanksgiving Day.

Nick and his wife tied the knot in 2012 and are proud parents of two boys and adorable, miracle twin babies. The entire family looked happy as they posed for a sweet family photo that will forever be a reminder of how far and how well they've done in life.

In his post, Vujicic who shared the photo on his social media page crowded it with an appreciation for life, family and everything that makes his life pleasant. Many find his post inspiring and well-thought out.
