Is Rochelle Humes joining Coronation Street?

Publish date: 2024-08-29

    Rochelle Humes was all smiles as she stood outside Coronation Street's famous Rovers Return Inn pub but all is not as it seems. Although the 27-year-old looked ready to start her acting career when she posed on the show's famous cobbled streets, she was quick to clarify she wouldn't be joining the cast.

    Rochelle posed outside 's Rovers Return Inn

    "Surreal morning with the crew filming on the cobbles... #newshow @itv ? (no I'm not taking up acting) ???," she captioned the photo on Friday.

    The Saturdays singer shared another fun snap of her pulling a pint behind the bar. "Anyone? ?," she wrote. It is unknown what she is currently filming but it could very well be another presenting venture for the TV personality.

    The TV star pretended to pull pints in the famous pub

    Rochelle joined the X Factor family last year, hosting the with Melvin Odoom. Taking over from Caroline Flack and Olly Murs, the pair felt at home in their new surroundings.

    "They [Caroline and Olly] have been really helpful and they've told us how it's worked years before," Melvin said, while Rochelle said it was made easier by the fact that they already knew the judges before they started filming.

    "It instantly wasn't like 'Oh no this is a new job and you don't know anyone' and 'We've got to go and interview some judges'. It was like 'Grimmy you're coming over here,'" Rochelle joked.

    Rochelle and husband Marvin visited Disneyland Paris

    As well as her presenting roles and launching her ninth range for Very.co.uk, the mum-of-one recently spent the day at Disneyland Paris with husband Marvin Humes, joining children from three London charities for a special outing.

    Flying on a specially chartered British Airways jet for their day out, the youngsters enjoyed a personal 'meet and greet' with some of their favourite Disney characters – including the world-famous Mickey and Minnie Mouse – before having lunch with the stars and then spending the afternoon having a go on some of the rides before watching the magical parade.

    And it wasn't just the children who took advantage of all that Disneyland had to offer. Marvin and Rochelle took the opportunity to show their daughter Alaia-Mai, who will turn three in May, around the park. The adorable couple posed in front of Sleeping Beauty's Castle in the middle of the resort before later meeting Daisy Duck.
