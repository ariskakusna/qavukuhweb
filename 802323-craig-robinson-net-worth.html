Craig Robinson Net Worth 2024: What Is The Comedic Actor Worth?

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Craig Robinson, with his infectious charm and multifaceted talent, stands out as a beloved figure in the entertainment industry. With a net worth of $6 million in 2024, according to CelebrityNetWorth, Robinson's career encompasses an impressive range of roles in television, film, and stand-up comedy. His journey from a music teacher to a celebrated actor and comedian is a testament to his versatility and dedication to his craft.

Born on October 25, 1971, in Chicago, Illinois, Craig Phillip Robinson initially pursued a career in education, earning a degree in Music Education from Illinois State University and a Master of Education from Saint Xavier University. While teaching music in Chicago public schools, Robinson honed his comedic skills by performing stand-up comedy at local clubs. His big break came in the early 2000s when he moved to Los Angeles to pursue comedy full-time, quickly gaining recognition for his unique style and captivating stage presence.

Robinson's breakthrough role came in 2005 when he was cast as Darryl Philbin on the hit television series The Office. His portrayal of the warehouse foreman with a dry wit and endearing personality made him a fan favorite, cementing his status as a comedic force. The success of The Office opened doors to numerous opportunities in both television and film.
