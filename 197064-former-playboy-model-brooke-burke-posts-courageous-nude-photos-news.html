Former Playboy Model Brooke Burke Posts &quot;Courageous&quot; Nude Photos

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Brooke Burke took a risk when she posted a racy photo on her social media profiles but it ended up working in her favor with most of her fans praising her for it. The 47-year-old has been out of the limelight for a minute, gaining fame as a Playboy model. At that time in her life, she was certainly comfortable getting a little sexy for the cameras but as she aged, she decided that it was less appropriate. That all changed yesterday when the actress and model went bottomless in a new photoshoot, building up the courage to post it online.

The model got on her workout gear but instead of suiting up completely, she went without her leggings or underwear, opting to pose nude from the waist down. She leans against the wall and smiles big, showing off her toned figure and confidently reminding everybody of her beauty.

"Going for it!" said Burke with a grin. "It took me a bit to get the courage to post this female curated shoot which represents many things for me. Vulnerability, sensuality, confidence. Yaaasss we can be sexy strong and YES you can tone, sculpt & reshape your beautiful body at every age."

The mother of four has shared her workout regimen with the world, according to Yahoo, and you can see that she's been staying hard at work with one look at her athletic frame.
