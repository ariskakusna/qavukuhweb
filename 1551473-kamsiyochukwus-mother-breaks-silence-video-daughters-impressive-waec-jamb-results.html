Kamsiyochukwu Umeh's Mother Breaks Silence in Video After Her Daughter's Impressive WASSCE, UTME Res

Publish date: 2024-08-10
  • Chinyero Umeh, the mother of Kamsiyochukwu Umeh, has finally spoken out after her daughter’s remarkable WASSCE and UTME results
  • She expressed her joy and pride in her daughter, who scored the highest mark in UTME
  • She also revealed that Kamsiyochukwu had a passion for reading from a young age and that they, as parents, did everything they could to support her

Chinyero Umeh, the proud mother of Kamsiyochukwu Umeh, has finally broken her silence after her daughter’s outstanding performance in this year's WASSCE and UTME.

In an interview, she shared her joy and gratitude, praising her daughter for achieving the highest score in the UTME, a highly competitive and prestigious test for Nigerian students.

Kamsiyochukwu's mother speaks

She also disclosed details about her daughter’s background and personality. The proud mother revealed that Kamsiyochukwu had always shown a keen interest and love for reading since she was a little girl.

As supportive parents, she said they did everything in their power to nurture and encourage her daughter’s passion for learning and excellence.

She said:

"I am very proud of my daughter's achievements. It is indeed a thing of pride for everyone. Kamsiyochukwu has always been a voracious reader from a tender age, and when we discovered that, we decided to support her with everything we can."

Kamsiyochukwu made history by scoring the highest mark -- 360 --in this year's UTME.

This national examination determines the admission of students into Nigerian universities.

She scored an impressive 365 out of 400.

She also excelled in the WAEC, another national examination that assesses the academic achievement of secondary school students.

She obtained A1 in eight subjects, including English, Mathematics, Chemistry, Biology, and Religious Studies.
