Are you planning a light meal before your Christmas feast and wondering if Firehouse Subs is open on Christmas this year? Let’s find out about Firehouse Subs’ Christmas schedule.

Firehouse Subs Takes a Holiday Break

Firehouse Subs, known for its delicious subs and inspired by the firefighting background of its founders, will be closed on Christmas Day. While they’re usually open on many holidays, they’ve decided to give their hardworking employees a break on significant days like Christmas, Thanksgiving, and Easter.

A Brief Look at Firehouse Subs

Founded in 1994 by two firefighter brothers, Firehouse Subs has grown into a popular fast-food restaurant chain. With a unique theme inspired by firefighting, the chain has expanded rapidly, now boasting over 1200 locations worldwide, including Canada and Puerto Rico.

Delicious Subs with a Kick

Firehouse Subs offers a wide variety of subs, all served with a generous helping of hot sauce. Their use of extra mayonnaise balances out the heat, creating a delicious sandwich that has won the hearts of many customers.
