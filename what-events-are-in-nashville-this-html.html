What Events Are In Nashville This Weekend

Publish date: 2024-08-09
People are currently reading this guide.

Nashville Weekend: Shindig or Snoozefest? Let's Find Out!

Howdy, y'all! Fixin' to hit Music City this weekend but worried it'll be flatter than a Grand Ole Opry singer's hat on a Tuesday afternoon? Worry not, sugar! Nashville's got more goin' on than bachelorette parties and hot chicken (though, let's be real, there's always plenty of both).

Brick Fanatics Unite!

Calling all LEGO lovers! This weekend, the Nashville Fairgrounds transforms into a plastic brick paradise with the Brick Fan Expo. Build your wildest creations, meet LEGO masters, and maybe even snag a rare minifigure (we won't judge if you hide it in your suitcase). Warning: Stepping on a rogue LEGO barefoot might put a damper on your weekend. Consider sensible footwear.

Festival Foodie Fun!

Spice up your life (sorry, not sorry, Spice Girls) with the 2024 Tomat-O-Rama Festival at the Nashville Farmers' Market. This ain't your average tomato pageant, folks. We're talkin' heirloom beauties, juicy romas, and tastings that'll have you saying "tomato about that!" Pro tip: Wear clothes that hide ketchup stains easily (because let's face it, tomato fights are basically inevitable).

From Drag Queens to Dance Parties: Something for Everyone! ️‍睊

Channel your inner Taylor Swift at the Taylor Swift Drag Brunch at Zanies Nashville. Belt out classics, revel in outrageous costumes, and prepare to have your weekend pre-gamed with a healthy dose of sass. Not a Swiftie? No worries! There's also a Taylor Swift Laser Dance Party happening for those who prefer their TayTay with a touch of neon.

Looking for something a little more low-key? Catch a free flick under the stars at Movies After Dark or explore the historic Monroe Tour of Homes.

Still on the Fence?

Fear not, friend! Nashville's got options for every kind of weekend warrior. Catch some live music at a rooftop bar, lose yourself in the Grand Ole Opry's timeless charm, or challenge your knowledge at trivia night (there's one happening at practically every other bar!).

The point is, Nashville this weekend is anything but a snoozefest. So, grab your boots, your dancing shoes, or your pajamas (hey, self-care is important too!), and get ready for a good time!

How-To Nashville Weekend Survival Guide:

  • How to avoid a bachelorette party pile-up? Stick to the side streets and avoid the areas around Honky Tonk Row.
  • How to navigate the heat? Hydrate like a champion (and by champion, we mean competitive eater at a chili cook-off).
  • How to find the best hot chicken? Ask a local (but be prepared for passionate opinions!).
  • How to score free entertainment? Many museums offer free admission on certain days, and there's always live music happening somewhere in the city.
  • How to avoid a hangover? Pace yourself, cowboy (or cowgirl)!
