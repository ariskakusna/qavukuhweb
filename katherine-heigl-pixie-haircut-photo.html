Katherine Heigl Just Chopped Her Hair Into a Pixie

Publish date: 2024-08-12

Bye-bye bob! Katherine Heigl got a little scissor-happy this weekend when she paid a visit to her mane man David Babaii, who transformed her chin-skimming style into a pixie crop. "It's hard to explain the love we have for this beauty. She's a friend, she's an animal activist, she's an amazing talent, and now she's rock'n a new killer short cut," Babaii captioned a photo of Heigl's new 'do. "Today we had a blast like we always do!" The star has been getting pretty experimental with her look lately, and last month, she gave her blonde strands a deeper brunette tint.
