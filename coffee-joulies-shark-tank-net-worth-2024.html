The Founders of Coffee Joulies

Dave Petrillo and Dave Jackson, the inventors of Coffee Joulies, are mechanical engineers who turned their passion for coffee into a successful business venture. Their backgrounds in engineering helped them design and perfect the Coffee Joulies, which are small, bean-shaped devices that regulate the temperature of hot beverages.

How Coffee Joulies Work

Coffee Joulies are designed with a phase change material inside that melts at 140 degrees Fahrenheit. When placed in hot coffee, the Joulies absorb excess heat, cooling the coffee to a drinkable temperature faster. As the coffee cools naturally, the material begins to solidify again, releasing heat back into the coffee to keep it warm longer.
