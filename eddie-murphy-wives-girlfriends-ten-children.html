Publish date: 2024-08-24

EDDIE Murphy has starred in a number of of legendary comedies including Coming to America and Trading Places.

How many times has Eddie Murphy been married?

Eddie Murphy has been married once, but has been in several relationships.

He was married to Nicole Mitchell from 1993 until their divorce in 2006.

He then dated Scary Spice, Mel B, from 2006-2007 and Tracy Edmonds in 2008.

The comedian is currently engaged to Australian actress Paige Butcher.

How many kids does Eddie Murphy have?

The actor has 10 children with five different women.

Paulette McNeely is the mother of his first born son, Eric Murphy.

Ex-wife Nicole whom he was married to for 13 years, gave birth to five of his children: Bria, Myles, Shayne, Zola and Bella.

Mel B is the mother of his daughter Angel.

Paige Butcher gave birth to her and Eddie's first child together, a daughter named Izzy Oona Murphy, in 2016.

In August 2018, Eddie's rep confirmed that the couple were expecting their second child - Eddie's tenth, a son named Max Charles Murphy.

“I really got lucky with my kids,” Eddie told People magazine in 2016.

“There really isn’t a bad one in the bunch, everyone turned out to be really good people.”
