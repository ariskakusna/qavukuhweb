World Cup 2022: This is how an Argentine woman fixed her Dibu Martinez tattoo that had a glaring err

Publish date: 2024-08-16

An Argentina fan decided to get a tattoo with the name of Emiliano 'Dibu' Martinez on her back after his heroics in the 2022 World Cup final against France.

The Aston Villa goalkeeper produced an extraordinary save to deny Randal Kolo Muani late in extra time, sending the game to a penalty shootout in which he saved one spot kick to lead Argentina to glory.

Following the final, Patricia took to social media to post a picture of a tattoo on her back that included a pair of gloves, an Argentine flag, three stars for the Albiceleste's three World Cup trophies and the name of the Villa keeper.

However, either her tattoo artist made a mistake and wrote 'Fernandez' instead of 'Martinez' or she had asked for the wrong tattoo.

This is how she solved the incorrect Dibu Martinez tattoo

The woman was forced to correct the tattoo and, a few days later, she posted a picture of the new tattoo.

While the gloves, flag and stars remained intact, 'Dibu Fernandez' was replaced by a goalkeeper's figure making a save and 'Dibu Martinez' was written on the side.

Other details from the Dibu Martinez tattoo

The tattoo also included his famous phrase "Mira que to como", which means "Careful, I'll get you".

Martinez said that to Yerry Mina during the penalty shootout against Colombia in the previous Copa America.
