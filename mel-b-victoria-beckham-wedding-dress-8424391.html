Mel B Revealed the Sweet Way Victoria Beckham Is Involved In Her Upcoming Wedding

Publish date: 2024-08-18

Mel B (formally Melanie Brown) and Victoria Beckham are proof that friendships really never end, because Brown just revealed the super sweet way that her longtime pal and fellow Spice Girls alumna is involved in her wedding.

During an appearance on Today with Hoda & Jenna, Mel shared that Beckham will be designing her dress for her upcoming nuptials to fiancé Rory McPhee. Lest you not forget that her oh-so-posh bandmate has her own eponymous fashion brand.

"Victoria actually designed my dress and my mom’s dress," she shared on Monday's episode. "It’s such a beautiful honor to get."

Of course, as a certified Y2K fashion idol, Mel B (affectionately known as Scary Spice) can't just have one gown on her special day. In fact, she plans on going big (and not going home) with a minimum of three different dresses. "I don't think it would be fair to just have one," she told hosts Hoda Kotb and Jenna Bush Hager (spoken like a true pop star). "Just have as many as you want — at least three, right?"

"I'm going to really go for it," she continued. "One to actually get married in, which is the proper ceremony — very proper at this church, St. Paul’s [Cathedral]. And then maybe I want to change out of that and be a bit more less… You know, I actually don't know!"

It makes perfect sense that Brown will need several dresses fit for a princess: she and McPhee are tying the knot at St. Paul’s, the same church where Princess Diana and Prince Charles had their 1981 wedding. She scored the venue thanks to her status as an MBE (Member of the Order of the British Empire), which she received in 2022.

"So I got an MBE for all my charity work that I’ve been doing and creating awareness for all that kind of stuff," she explained.
