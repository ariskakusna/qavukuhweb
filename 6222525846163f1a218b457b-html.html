Colby Covington Net Worth: How much is the UFC champion earning in 2022?

Publish date: 2024-08-13

Colby Covington is the best UFC fighter ranked in the welterweight category.

Covington is 34 years old. He was born in Clovis, California, and began wrestling in Thurston High School in Oregon.

During his high school years, Covington won the state championship as a senior.

Colby Covington went to Iowa Central Community College where he won the 165 lbs national championship in his first year.

He shared a room with Jon Jones, the future UFC champion.

Covington moved back to Oregon and continued his college wrestling career at Oregon State University. He was a two-time All-American and double Pac-10 champion.

Colby Covington made his MMA debut on February 11, 2012, where he faced Chris Ensley at Midtown Throwdown 3. He won the fight via TKO in the 1st round.

Colby Covington debuted in the UFC in 2014. He fought Hunglow Wang on UFC Fight Night 48, and Covington won the fight by submission.

He fought Wagner Silva, Mike Pyle, Warlley Alves, Alex Garcia, Max Griffin, Bryan Barberena, Dong Hyun Kim, Demian Maia, Rafael dos Anjos, and Robbie Lawler.

Colby Covington's Net Worth

Reports say Colby Covington has earned $2,411,000 in his UFC career.

He has sponsorships with brands like Reebok, My Bookie, Alienware, and Bang Energy.

Covington has a 16-3 MMA record. Nevertheless, people know him better for his comments, tirades, and trash talks against his opponents.

Covington called out NBA superstar LeBron James after his win over Tyron Woodley, even saying LeBron won't last 10 seconds against him in the octagon.

Public opinion describe Covington as the "super villain" of the UFC.
