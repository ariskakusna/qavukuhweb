Avery Matera - InStyle

Publish date: 2024-08-06

Title: Contributing Writer

Education: Tufts University

Location: New York City, New York

Expertise: Fashion, Beauty, Celebrity

  • Avery Matera is a writer and photographer based in New York City who covers fashion, style, beauty, and interior design.
  • After working on the French market at Glamour Magazine, she is now a freelance journalist and photographer, traveling as much and as often as possible.
  • Her work can be seen in many national publications, including InStyle, Glamour, W Magazine, The Zoe Report, Refinery29, and Food + Wine.

Experience

Avery started her editorial career as a Fashion Assistant for Glamour in Paris, where she was the editor for their swimwear and activewear lines. Her experience also includes creating branded content for Conde Nast and Hearst, as well as a contributing writer for many publications, including InStyle. She was also the daily fashion and beauty news editor for Teen Vogue.

Education

Avery earned her Bachelor of Arts in English from Tufts University.
