Ian Harold Woosnam OBE (born 2 March 1958) is a Welsh professional golfer who competes on the European Tour. Woosnam, often known as ‘Woosie,’ ‘Woosers,’ or the ‘Wee Welshman,’ was a member of the “Big Five” group of European golfers, all of whom were born within a year of one another and have all won major championships as well as helped Europe become competitive in the Ryder Cup. This group included Seve Ballesteros, Nick Faldo, Bernhard Langer, and Sandy Lyle, all of whom he considered to be his equals.
