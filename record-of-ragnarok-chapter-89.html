Plotlines & Story

Record of Ragnarok is a manga series that follows the story of a tournament between 13 gods and 13 humans, who are chosen by the Valkyries, the maidens of war. The tournament is held to decide the fate of humanity, which the gods have decided to exterminate due to their corruption and wickedness.

The rules of the tournament are simple: each fight is a one-on-one match, and the first side to win seven matches will be the victor. The humans have the advantage of using the Volund, a special weapon that is created by the fusion of a human soul and a Valkyrie. The Volund grants the human fighter the power to harm the gods, who are otherwise invulnerable to mortal weapons.
