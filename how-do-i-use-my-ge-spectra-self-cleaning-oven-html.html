How do I use my GE Spectra self cleaning oven?

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Answer

By pressing the “Self Clean” button on the touch pad, you may programme your oven to clean itself. Then choose your preferred cleaning time, which should be between three and five hours. Automatically set to four hours on all Spectra models, this feature is available on all models. To change the time on most models, you must choose either — or + from the drop-down menu.

Ovens That Clean Themselves Heat is used to clean the oven, and temperatures above normal cooking temperatures are used. We do not recommend leaving the oven racks in the oven during the self-clean cycle. The racks may warp and discolour due to the extreme heat created during this cycle. The racks can also damage the rack guides of the porcelain oven cavity due to expansion and contraction.
