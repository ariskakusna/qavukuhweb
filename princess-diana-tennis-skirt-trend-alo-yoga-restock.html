Tennis skirts and TikTok basically go hand-in-hand at this point. Personally, I've been obsessed with the skirt style since before I even downloaded the app, mostly because Princess Diana was a fan of the trend, and well, she is Princess Diana.

At the beginning of the pandemic, I opted to wear tennis skirts instead of sweatpants because I am an incredibly Extra person who could just not fathom the idea of not getting dressed up everyday. But truth be told, I wish the famous Alo Yoga tennis skirt was around then because it's just as cute (but obviously far more comfortable for lounging) than the American Apparel options I bought from Amazon.
