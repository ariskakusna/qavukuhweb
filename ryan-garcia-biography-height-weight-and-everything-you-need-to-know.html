Ryan Garcia Biography, Height, Weight, And Everything You Need To Know

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Born in Victorville, California, on August 8, 1998, Ryan Garcia is a professional boxer who competes in the United States. He is renowned for his lightning-quick reflexes and devastating punches. What you should know about Ryan Garcia is as follows:

Ryan Garcia Height and Weight

Ryan Garcia is approximately 135 lbs (61 kg) in weight and 5 feet 10 inches (178 cm) tall.

Ryan Garcia Childhood and Career

At the age of 7, Ryan Garcia started boxing, following in the footsteps of his father, a former professional fighter. He won multiple national titles throughout his prosperous amateur career. At the age of 18, he began his professional career in 2016.

Ryan Garcia Career

Ryan Garcia has compiled a record of 21 wins, 0 losses, and 18 knockouts since going pro. The WBC Silver lightweight title, the WBO NABO lightweight title, and the WBA interim lightweight title are just a few of the titles he has won. He fought Luke Campbell in January 2021 for the interim lightweight championship of the WBC and won by TKO in the seventh round.

Ryan Garcia Personal Life

Ryan Garcia is of Irish and Mexican ancestry. Together with Catherine Gamez, his ex-girlfriend, he has a daughter named Rylie.

Ryan Garcia Controversies

Throughout his career, Ryan Garcia has been embroiled in a number of scandals, including a highly publicized dispute with fellow boxer Gervonta Davis and allegations of domestic violence made by his ex-girlfriend.

Ryan Garcia Social Media

On social media sites like Instagram, where he has over 10 million followers, Ryan Garcia has a sizable fan base. He is renowned for having a showy personality and frequently gives his admirers a peek into his personal life.
