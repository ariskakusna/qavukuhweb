90 Day Fianc Season 7 Where Are They Now?

Publish date: 2024-08-19

The seventh season of “90 Day Fiancé” captivated audiences with its mix of romance, drama, and the inevitable cultural clashes that come with international love stories. As the couples navigated the K-1 visa process and their 90-day journey to the altar, viewers were left wondering which relationships would stand the test of time. Now, we delve into where the Season 7 couples are today, providing an update on their lives since the cameras stopped rolling.

Introduction to Season 7 Couples

Season 7 of “90 Day Fiancé” introduced viewers to a diverse group of couples, each with their unique challenges and love stories. From age differences to language barriers and skeptical families, these couples faced it all. Let’s take a look at where they are now.

Michael Jessen and Juliana Custodio

Michael, a wealthy wine entrepreneur from Connecticut, and Juliana, a young Brazilian model, raised eyebrows with their significant age gap. Despite the skepticism, they tied the knot in a lavish ceremony. Today, they have faced some challenges but remain together, navigating life as a married couple.

Tania Maduro and Syngin Colchester

Tania and Syngin’s journey was filled with ups and downs, including Tania’s trip to Costa Rica for a month during Syngin’s first 90 days in the U.S. Despite their rocky road, they got married. However, recent updates suggest that they may be facing marital issues.

Emily Larina and Sasha Larin

Emily, from Oregon, met Sasha, a personal trainer from Russia, while teaching English abroad. They welcomed a son before moving to the U.S. The couple married and are currently living in the States, focusing on family life and Sasha’s career.

Anna Campisi and Mursel Mistanoglu

Anna, a beekeeper from Nebraska, and Mursel, also a beekeeper from Turkey, had a language barrier and cultural differences to overcome. They married in a touching ceremony and are now running a business together, proving that love can overcome any obstacle.

Mike Youngquist and Natalie Mordovtseva

Mike, from Washington, and Natalie, from Ukraine, had a tumultuous relationship marked by religious differences and trust issues. They married but have had a rocky relationship since. Their current status is one of the most speculated among fans.

Robert Springs and Anny Francisco

Robert, a single dad from Florida, and Anny, from the Dominican Republic, had a quick engagement after meeting online. They married and have since expanded their family, sharing their life updates on social media.

Blake Abelard and Jasmin Lahtinen

Blake, a music producer from Los Angeles, and Jasmin, from Finland, faced doubts about the authenticity of their relationship. They married and are still together, with Jasmin adjusting to life in the U.S. and Blake continuing his music career.

Life After the 90 Days

After the 90 days are up and the final episode airs, fans are often left wondering what happens to the couples they’ve grown to love—or love to hate. Here’s a closer look at the lives of the Season 7 couples post-show.

Michael and Juliana: Business Ventures and Family Life

Michael and Juliana have been focusing on their entrepreneurial endeavors while balancing family life. They’ve faced some criticism but continue to share their love story on social media.

Tania and Syngin: Marital Struggles and Personal Growth

Tania and Syngin have been open about their marital struggles, with both parties pursuing personal growth. They’ve appeared on “90 Day Fiancé: Happily Ever After?” to share more of their story.

Emily and Sasha: Fitness Goals and Family Focus

Emily and Sasha have been concentrating on Sasha’s fitness career and raising their son. They’ve managed to stay out of the spotlight, focusing on building a stable life in the U.S.

Anna and Mursel: Beekeeping Business and Cultural Integration

Anna and Mursel have successfully merged their love for beekeeping into a business. They’ve also worked on Mursel’s integration into American culture and Anna’s family.

Mike and Natalie: Marital Tensions and Uncertain Future

Mike and Natalie’s relationship has been one of the most dramatic, with ongoing tensions and an uncertain future. Fans are eager to see if they can reconcile their differences.

Robert and Anny: Family Expansion and Social Media Presence

Robert and Anny have welcomed a new addition to their family and are active on social media, where they share updates about their lives and interact with fans.

Blake and Jasmin: Music and Modelling Careers

Blake and Jasmin continue to pursue their respective careers in music and modeling. They’ve faced skepticism but remain committed to proving their critics wrong.
