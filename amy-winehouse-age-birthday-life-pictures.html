Remembering Amy Winehouse on Her Birthday

Publish date: 2024-08-15

Today, we’re honoring the life and legacy of Amy Winehouse, who would have celebrated her 33nd birthday today. The remarkable talent rose to stardom in the early 2000s, awing fans with both her soulful voice and retro fashion sense. From her perfect cat eye to her beehive ‘do, this star was truly one of a kind.

Winehouse became a household name with her 2006 album, Back to Black, which took home five Grammy awards and gave us the chart-topping hit, “Rehab.” Fans latched onto her signature style and musical genius, but sadly her complicated private life overshadowed her bright future.

Winehouse tragically died on July 23, 2011 at the young age of 27. Today, on her birthday, we’re paying tribute to the star with a look back at her life in photographs. In the stunning raw images from PROVOKR.com, Winehouse laughs, primps, and goofs around, exhibiting the fun-loving humor that we loved her for.
