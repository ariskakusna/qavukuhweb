Sarah Jessica Parker Wears An All-White Outfit With Babydoll Shoes

Publish date: 2024-08-19

Everyone knows that fashion rules don't really exist. They're pointless; truly, just wear whatever you like. That being said, the one "rule" people really seem to get worked up over is the whole "no white after Labor Day" thing. Thankfully, Labor Day is still a couple months away, and Sarah Jessica Parker seems to be wearing as many all-white outfits as possible before the time comes to pack them up.

Jennifer Lopez, who often wears plenty of white outfits after Labor Day, has been wearing a lot of all-white outfits recently, too. It's a practical summer fashion decision that'll help keep you cooler than an all-black look. (Parker still kept the look very her, though, in a pair of black babydoll shoes.)

Lopez and Parker have both also recently opted for white jeans, which may just be having an unexpected comeback while the rest of the internet is arguing over low-rise and skinny styles. White jeans are just as easy to wear as denim, but they feel less expected and more fun.

If you're not willing to get into the heated denim debates of 2021, white jeans might just be your new go-to. If style icons like SJP and JLo are wearing them nonstop, clearly they're about to be everywhere. Nordstrom has a handful of white jeans that are under $50, and some for as little as $36. And if you want to play by the fashion rulebook, you still have plenty of chances to wear them before September 6.
