Publish date: 2024-08-30

FRONTIER is a popular low-cost airline that is headquartered in Denver, Colorado.

Because the airline has lower costs than most, the cities it flies to are limited, causing many to wonder where they actually go.

Where does Frontier fly?

In the United States alone, there are over 19,000 airports.

However, Frontier only flies to some of those locations and does not have stops in all 50 states.
