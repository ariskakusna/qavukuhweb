Jacob Elordi's Bottega Veneta Bag Moment Was a Euphoric Flex

Publish date: 2024-08-22

"Cassie, how long has Nate Jacobs been nailing the bag game?" Or whatever Rue said.

Euphoria star Jacob Elordi has quite the bag collection — more like a bag paradise — comprised of Burberry, Fendi, Celine, and Bottega Veneta murses.

Last year, the actor delivered lots of bag action during the Fall/Winter 2022 presentations, a.k.a. Euphoria's fashion month. And now, Elordi's handbag heaven is only getting better, thanks to his latest style moment.

www.instagram.com/p/CcbzLa1PzHO/

While running errands on March 30, Elordi flexed Bottega Veneta's medium-sized Andiamo bag tucked crook of his elbow and what looked like a matcha drink in hand. A nook-of-the-arm Bottega flex? Elordi gets it.

As for the rest of Elordi's 'fit, he wore a green sweatshirt layered over a washed grey t-shirt paired with some tiny, black shorts, a white beanie, and his now-signature stash. On foot, the actor opted for Fear of God's California slide sandals, which he later swapped out for a pair of green adidas x Gucci Gazelle sneakers.

The verdict for his ensemble is guilty of being cozy with counts of subtle flexes.

It's safe to say Elordi's got a thing for Bottega's bags, and understandably so. Whether it be from Daniel Lee or Matthieu Blazy's era, Bottega Veneta's accessories are skillfully-crafted hits.

In addition to his Andiamo bag, Elordi was also spotted with the Cassette bag while he sported a Strand tote in the streets of Los Angeles last year.

On top of his love affair with the house's handbags, the Tag Heuer ambassador also attended the Bottega Veneta's presentations in the past. Could we see an Bottega ambassador deal in the future? I hope so, but only time will tell.

Until then, catch us happily soaking in the euphoric goodness of Jacob Elordi's handbag utopia.
