This Guy Dey Sing: Singer Dija Features Portable on Love Song, His Vocals Leave Fans Gushin

Publish date: 2024-08-25
  • Nigerian singer, Portable, caught the attention of fans with his recent collaboration with Di'ja
  • The former Mavin Records signee featured the Zazu Zeh crooner on a love song on her album
  • A number of netizens reacted to the new track on social media and praised Portable for being versatile

One of Nigeria's singers, Habeeb Okikiola, aka Portable, trended online following his feature on former Mavin signee, Hadiza Blell, aka Di'ja's album.

In a post shared on their social media pages, Di'ja's album tracklist showed that she had featured Portable on one of the songs.

The song, titled Never Give Up, had the Zazu crooner singing a verse about love, and it surprised some netizens.

This came a few hours after Portable gifted a hypewoman who sang about him N1 million.
