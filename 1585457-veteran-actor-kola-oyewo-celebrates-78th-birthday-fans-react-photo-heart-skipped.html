Veteran Actor Kola Oyewo Celebrates 78th Birthday, Fans React to Photo: My Heart Skipped at First

Publish date: 2024-08-19
  • Veteran Nollywood actor Kola Oyewo has now clocked 78, to the joy of many fans
  • A photo of the movie star was posted on social media, and netizens reacted to the news of his birthday
  • Several fans were pleased to see that it wasn’t another news of a Nollywood loss as they celebrated Oyewo

Famous Nollywood actor Kola Oyewo recently celebrated his 78th birthday to the joy of his numerous fans.

The much-loved movie star turned the new age on March 27, 2024, and many of his admirers celebrated him on social media.

A photo of the movie star was shared on Instagram by celebrity blogger Temilola Sobola, and netizens reacted to the post.

Kola Oyewo and Fatomilola's friendship

Meanwhile, Legit.ng earlier reported that Nigerian actor Kunle Afod continued to pay surprise visits to veteran movie stars in the Yoruba movie industry, and Kola Oyewo and Peter Fatomila were his hosts.

Afod, who made headlines over his visit to Baba Wande, posted a video of his time with the veterans in Osun state.

Oyewo recounted how the doctor informed him he had a large prostate, which was hindering him from passing out urine. In another clip towards the end of the video, Oyewo revealed he and Peter Fatomilola had been friends for more than 51 years, recounting how they first met in Osogbo.
