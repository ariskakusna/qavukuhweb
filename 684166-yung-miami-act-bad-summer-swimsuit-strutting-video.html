Yung Miami Sets The Tone For &quot;Act Bad&quot; Summer In A Super Tiny Swimsuit

Publish date: 2024-08-04

When the City Girls and Diddy teamed up with Fabolous to drop the "Act Bad" music video earlier this month, many were surprised to see the father of six getting wilder than Yung Miami and JT for the camera. In the time since the catchy potential song of the summer landed on DSPs, we've also seen Caresha step out with Sean Combs to sit courtside for an NBA game. At the time their famous faces were seen on the jumbotron, aptly naming them "Mr. & Mrs. 'Act Bad'."

They were dripping in designer duds while at the game, but in her free time, Miami is making it clear what her vibes are for the coming months. From the looks of things, the rap diva will be spending plenty of time outside, showing off her bodacious body. Her Twitter profile has already been lighting up with videos of her throwing it back in the club at 3 AM and salacious, flirtatious messages to Moneybagg Yo's partner Ari Fletcher. On Monday (May 29), she turned up the heat some more, sharing a clip of her saucily strutting through what looks to be a luxurious hotel pool area.

Yung Miami Reminds Us How to "Act Bad"

The mother of two wore nothing more than an ultra-tiny black one-piece swimsuit, the words "Act Bad" written across her chest. While Caresha's front is fully under wraps, nearly her entire backside is on display in the thong-cut garment. "God d*mn! Miami said, 'That's a motherf*cking thong, b*tch!'" a friend recording behind the camera yells out. The City Girl nonchalantly walks past two people while showing off her goods, both of whom awkwardly stroll away as she proudly lets her star power shine.

It remains unclear what else Caresha, Diddy, and their other collaborators have in store to promote their new single, but knowing them, it definitely won't be lowkey.
